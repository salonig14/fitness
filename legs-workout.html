Legs Workout

Develop strength, stability, and lower-body power.

Squats

Power and muscle for the lower body.

  1. Place feet shoulder-width apart and keep your chest up.
  2. Lower your hips until thighs are parallel or lower.
  3. Push through your heels to stand back up.

Lunges

Improve balance and mobility.

  1. Step forward with one leg and lower your back knee.
  2. Keep your torso upright and front knee aligned.
  3. Push back to the starting position and switch sides.

Romanian Deadlift

Targets hamstrings and glutes.

  1. Hold the weight in front of your thighs.
  2. Hinge at the hips and keep your back flat.
  3. Return by driving hips forward and squeezing glutes.

Calf Raises

Build lower-leg strength.

  1. Stand tall with feet flat and stable.
  2. Rise onto your toes as high as possible.
  3. Lower slowly back to the floor.
