Arms Workout

Build bigger, stronger biceps and triceps with focused arm training.

Bicep Curl

Grow the biceps with control.

  1. Stand tall with dumbbells at your sides.
  2. Curl the weight up without moving your elbows.
  3. Lower the dumbbells slowly.

Hammer Curl

Build forearms and arm thickness.

  1. Hold dumbbells with palms facing each other.
  2. Curl the weights toward your shoulders.
  3. Control the lowering phase.

Tricep Dips

Develop pressing strength and triceps.

  1. Place hands on a bench or parallel bars.
  2. Lower your body by bending the elbows.
  3. Press back up using the triceps.

Skull Crushers

Target the triceps directly.

  1. Lie down holding dumbbells or an EZ bar above you.
  2. Bend the elbows to lower the weight near your forehead.
  3. Extend the arms back up.
