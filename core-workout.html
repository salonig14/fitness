Core Workout

Strengthen your abs and lower back for balance and stability.

Plank

Full core control and bracing.

  1. Place forearms on the floor and keep your body straight.
  2. Brace your core and hold your hips level.
  3. Maintain steady breathing throughout the hold.

Dead Bug

Deep abdominal strength and stability.

  1. Lie on your back with arms up and knees bent.
  2. Extend opposite arm and leg slowly.
  3. Return to center and switch sides.

Mountain Climbers

Build endurance and core engagement.

  1. Start in a plank position.
  2. Drive knees toward your chest one at a time.
  3. Keep your hips low and core tight.

Leg Raises

Focus on lower abs.

  1. Lie flat and keep hands under your hips.
  2. Lift both legs up slowly without swinging.
  3. Lower them with control before touching the floor.
