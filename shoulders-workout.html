Shoulders Workout

Shape strong shoulders with pressing and isolation work.

Overhead Press

Mass and power for shoulders.

  1. Start with the weight at shoulder height.
  2. Press straight up until arms are fully extended.
  3. Lower back to the shoulders with control.

Lateral Raise

Build side delts and width.

  1. Hold dumbbells at your sides with soft elbows.
  2. Raise them out to shoulder height.
  3. Lower slowly without swinging.

Face Pull

Supports rear delt and posture health.

  1. Grab the rope with hands facing each other.
  2. Pull the rope toward your face with elbows high.
  3. Return slowly while keeping tension.

Arnold Press

Complete shoulder development.

  1. Begin with palms facing you at shoulder level.
  2. Rotate and press the dumbbells overhead.
  3. Reverse the motion on the way down.
