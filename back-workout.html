Back Workout

Improve pulling power, posture, and upper-back strength.

Pull-ups

Build width and body control.

  1. Grip the bar slightly wider than shoulder width.
  2. Pull your chest up toward the bar.
  3. Lower yourself under control to a full hang.

Lat Pulldown

Controlled pulling for lats.

  1. Hold the bar with a wide grip and sit tall.
  2. Pull the bar down to the upper chest.
  3. Slowly return the bar overhead.

Seated Row

Targets the mid-back muscles.

  1. Keep your chest up and spine neutral.
  2. Pull the handle toward your torso.
  3. Extend arms forward without losing posture.

Deadlift

Strengthens the full posterior chain.

  1. Stand with feet hip-width and grip the bar.
  2. Drive through your legs to lift the bar with a straight back.
  3. Lower it back down with control.
