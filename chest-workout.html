Chest Workout

Build a stronger chest with pressing and bodyweight movements.

Push-ups

Bodyweight strength and control.

  1. Place hands under shoulders and keep your body straight.
  2. Lower your chest until it is close to the floor.
  3. Press back up while keeping your core tight.

Bench Press

Build chest mass and pressing power.

  1. Lie on the bench with feet planted and shoulder blades tight.
  2. Lower the bar to mid-chest with control.
  3. Press the bar back up to full extension.

Incline Dumbbell Press

Targets the upper chest.

  1. Set the bench at a 30 to 45 degree angle.
  2. Lower the dumbbells slowly to chest level.
  3. Press up and squeeze the upper chest at the top.

Chest Fly

Improves stretch and isolation.

  1. Keep a slight bend in your elbows throughout the movement.
  2. Open your arms wide until you feel a chest stretch.
  3. Bring the dumbbells together over your chest.
